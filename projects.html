Merch Order Management System

This project is a terminal-based application developed in Python to manage customer orders for student council merchandise. It collects customer information, such as their name and contact details, and allows them to choose from a list of available merchandise items. The application then processes the orders and saves all the customer details, including their selected items, into a CSV file for easy tracking and future reference. The system ensures that each order is recorded accurately, helping to organize and streamline the ordering process for the council.

Since the project runs entirely in the terminal, it is lightweight and simple to use, making it ideal for quick deployments during student events. Additionally, the use of CSV files allows for easy data retrieval and manipulation when reviewing sales or generating reports.

TaxWise Freelance Tax Calculator

This project is a Python-based tool designed to help freelancers calculate their taxes using three different methods: the graduated income tax rate, the 8% flat income tax rate, and the Optional Standard Deduction.

Users can select their preferred method or compare all options to determine which best suits their situation. Additionally, the program includes a Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section, providing valuable insights and clarifications related to freelance tax-paying.

By offering multiple tax calculation methods and relevant information, this tool helps freelancers minimize their tax liability and make informed decisions on the best approach for their financial situation.
